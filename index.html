How to work with me

What works
  • Clear, stable requirements from the start
  • Structured processes and established workflows
  • Challenging problems that push me to learn
  • Time to think through complex design challenges
  • Opportunities to explore new tools and approaches
  • Context and background upfront so I can make informed decisions
What slows me down
  • Unclear or ambiguous requirements
  • Frequently changing requirements mid-stream
  • Lack of context about why we're solving this problem
  • Starting work before requirements are solidified
  • Ad-hoc processes without clear structure
  • Last-minute pivots that invalidate previous work
