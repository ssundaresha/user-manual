# Monopo Style

Dark, typographic, agency-inspired. Based on the aesthetic of Monopo London.

## Philosophy

- Black background, white text, minimal color
- Heavy use of uppercase and wide letter-spacing
- Grid-based layouts with visible borders
- Gradient accents used sparingly (warm orange to cool green)
- Minimal border-radius (2px max)
- Typography drives hierarchy, not color

## Key Properties

- **Font**: Space Grotesk (geometric, technical)
- **Background**: #000000
- **Text**: #ffffff (primary), #7f7f7f (secondary)
- **Borders**: #666970
- **Accent gradient**: radial from #cc490c through warm yellows to #65faca
- **Letter-spacing**: 0.15em (body), 0.03-0.05em (secondary text)
- **Border-radius**: 2px (everything)
- **Section padding**: 111px vertical
- **Gaps**: 25px (standard), 56px (large)

## Visual Characteristics

- Full-viewport hero with centered text
- "SCROLL" indicator with pulse animation at bottom
- Sections divided by 1px solid borders
- Cards have visible 1px borders, no fill
- Text is uppercase and widely spaced
- Bar charts use thin 3-4px bars
- Lists use no bullet markers, just border separators
- Fun facts box has a subtle gradient overlay at 5% opacity
- Footer is uppercase, subdued

## Mood

Confident. Technical. Gallery-like. The content breathes in large white space against the dark field. Nothing decorates — every element earns its place.
